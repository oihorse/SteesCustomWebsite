About

Time is beauty and quality is everything.

At Stees Design we disagree with the common mantra that “Time is money” and believe in going against the grain by taking “Time is beauty”. We begin with slowing down, listening to each customer, and assessing every last detail. Designing evolves to picking up the methodical chisel, embracing traditional methods with modern technologies, and thoughtfully taking extra steps that will produce Quality. Out of this patience, we can create something for you that has unique meaning and time honored beauty.

Here’s how it works:

Give us a call/text 410.698.5506, or email us at questions@steesdesign.com

Work with our design specialist to give your ideas an opportunity to take shape. We ensure that your vision, interests, and needs lead the way in the design process. The design specialist will assist in advising and give clear explanation of the creation process, and a quote.

We then sketch the out your plan, and once approved - we collect 1/3 of the total quote to begin building.

We build in our custom shop located in Havre de Grace, MD.

Delivery is arranged.
